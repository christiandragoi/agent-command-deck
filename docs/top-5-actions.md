# Top 5 Actions — Get First Paying Users

## Priority Order: Immediate Impact

### Action 1: Build and Demo to 10 Recruiting Agencies (50% Pilot Discount)
**Why:** Staffing agencies feel CV screening pain most acutely.
**Execution:**
1. Identify 10 agencies on LinkedIn specializing in tech/IT placements
2. Offer 4-week pilot at 50% off ($49.50 Starter, $149.50 Growth)
3. 15-min Zoom demo: upload CV → AI score → approve → done
4. Goal: 3 pilot customers signed
**Success metric:** 3 paid pilot customers

### Action 2: Publish 3 LinkedIn Articles
**Why:** Content builds authority; TA Directors search for solutions.
**Topics:**
1. "How AI cuts CV screening time by 80%" — data-driven before/after
2. "The real cost of manual candidate scoring" — 23h × $45 × 52wk = $53,820/yr wasted
3. "Why your ATS is not enough" — ATS stores, Agent Command Deck processes
**Success metric:** 500+ views, 10+ comments, 5 inbound demo requests

### Action 3: Cold Email 50 TA Directors with Personalized Loom
**Why:** Fastest path to a conversation.
**Execution:**
1. List of 50 TA Directors at 50–1,000 employee companies (tech, fintech, healthcare)
2. 60-second Loom showing: their job posting → drop mock CV → show score → approve
3. Email: 2 sentences + Loom thumbnail + Calendly link
**Email template:**
> Subject: Quick demo for [Company]'s TA team
> Hi [Name], I noticed you're hiring for [Role]. Our tool cuts CV screening from 23h/week to under 4h. I recorded a 60-sec demo: [Loom]. Happy to show you live. [Calendly]
**Success metric:** 15% reply rate, 5 demo bookings

### Action 4: Set Up Stripe and Make First $99 Subscription Live
**Why:** Nothing validates a product like real revenue.
**Checklist:**
- [ ] Stripe account verified
- [ ] Products created (Starter, Growth)
- [ ] Prices created (monthly + annual)
- [ ] Webhook endpoint configured
- [ ] Full checkout flow tested end-to-end
- [ ] LIVE MODE ENABLED
- [ ] **First real customer billed ($99)**
**Success metric:** First $99 in live revenue

### Action 5: Collect Testimonials from Pilot Users
**Why:** Social proof is #1 conversion driver for B2B SaaS.
**Execution:**
1. After week 2 of pilot: ask for specific metrics (before/after)
2. Record 30-sec video or get written quote
3. Add to landing page: carousel + stat bar + company logos
**Success metric:** 3 published testimonials + 1 case study
