## Below the Fold

### How It Works (3 Steps)
1. Upload CV (email forward, upload, or ATS sync)
2. AI Scores (parsed & scored against job req)
3. Recruiters Approve (one-click approve or auto-gen outreach)

Arrow connector between steps: Upload → AI Scores → Approve

### Features Grid (6 Cards)
1. Smart Intake — Upload, email forward, or webhook → parsed in seconds
2. AI Scoring — Scores CVs against job reqs with matching % & skill gaps
3. Kanban Board — Drag-drop prioritization, bulk actions
4. Team Workspace — Multi-user, role-based access, shared views
5. ATS Connect — Push approved candidates to Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby
6. Auto Outreach — One-click generate personalized email/InMail

### Pricing Preview (3 cards)
- Starter: $99/mo, 100 CVs, 1 user
- Growth: $299/mo, 500 CVs, 5 users, AI triage, "Popular" badge
- Enterprise: Custom, unlimited, everything + SSO/SLA
